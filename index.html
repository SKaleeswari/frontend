Evolution of Gold Bangles

Have you ever pondered on the evolution and antiquity of gold bangles? The love for this product line of gold has its origin in ancient civilizations. Wrist ornaments were used to adorn the wrists of all strata of people ranging from the most basic level of female workers to queens. In the history of bangles, several metals marked their presence but eventually declined leaving behind gold as the uncrowned king of bangles. Our nation has a tradition of fine and eclectic collection of gold bangles, across different empires and dynasties.
